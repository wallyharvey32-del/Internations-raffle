Terms, Conditions & Compliance

Ticket buyers must be 18 years of age or older and residents of Western Australia to purchase online.

Official Raffle Conditions

Raffle tickets are priced at $5.00 AUD per individual ticket. There is no limit on the number of tickets an eligible individual may buy. Purchases are restricted to persons aged 18 years and over. Tickets cannot be sold or transferred to minors.

Ticket numbers are pulled securely from our central register upon generation. Users may elect to draw sequential blocks or randomized combinations. Numbers are reserved temporarily during processing and permanently assigned only upon verified completion of payment confirmation.

The raffle will be drawn on the official date listed under the conditions of the WA Gaming permit. All winners will be notified within 2 business days via the email address and contact information provided during checkout. Results will be published proudly on our official Facebook portal. Prizes must be claimed within the legal statutory timeframe.

As this is a charitable fundraising initiative benefiting Charity Variety, all ticket transactions are final. No refunds will be issued for change of mind or operational adjustments, except where explicitly mandated under Western Australian consumer protection statutes.
