May 2026 Event

A Record-Breaking $21,000 Raised!

Our 48th annual tournament hit unparalleled heights at the Doubleview Bowling Club.

The Internations committee proudly presented a cheque to the value of $21,000 to Amy May from Variety Charity. This momentous contribution will deliver vital equipment, programs, and support systems directly to local kids and families navigating tough circumstances.

The Internations Committee with Amy May (Variety Charity)
2026 Champions

Australia Claims Victory

Superb greens and intense competition defined the matches at Doubleview.

Following a series of masterful displays, the multi-cultural fields narrowing down concluded with the Australian team sealing their names as the grand winners of the 2026 title. Our immense thanks go out to Doubleview Bowling Club for hosting a world-class weekend of sportsmanship.
